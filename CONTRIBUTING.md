# Contributing to Awesome Hackathons & Competitions 🎉

Thank you for helping make this resource better for bachelor's students around the world!

## How to Add a Competition

1. **Fork** this repository
2. **Edit** `README.md` — add your competition to the correct category table
3. Use this format:

```
| **Competition Name** | $X,XXX (1st place) | Annual | University students | [website.com](https://website.com) |
```

4. **Submit a Pull Request** with a title like: `Add [Competition Name] to [Category]`

## Requirements for Inclusion

- ✅ Prize of at least **$500 USD** (or equivalent)
- ✅ Open to **bachelor's/undergraduate students** (or open to all)
- ✅ Legitimate, established competition (1+ year running or well-funded inaugural)
- ✅ Official website is live and verifiable
- ✅ Not a scam / not predatory

## What to Include in Your PR

- Competition name (with official name spelling)
- Prize pool / amount for key placements
- Frequency (Annual, Bi-annual, Ongoing, etc.)
- Eligibility
- Official URL

## Reporting Outdated Info

If a competition has ended, changed prizes, or moved, please open an **Issue** and we'll update the listing.
